# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1][cc] as its code of conduct.

In short: we pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for
everyone. We welcome questions, disagreement, and critique offered in good faith
and with respect; we don't tolerate harassment, personal attacks, or
discrimination.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behaviour to the maintainers at **yijun.yu@gmail.com**.
Reports are handled confidentially. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject
contributions and comments that violate this code, and may ban contributors for
behaviour they deem inappropriate.

The full text, including enforcement guidelines, is at the link below.

[cc]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
